Welcome to Hilário de Sousa's webpage

Greetings! I am a linguist with a wide range of interests, with leanings towards the phonology, morphosyntax and semantic typology in East/Southeast Asian and Pacific languages. In this page you will find a list of publications (some downloadable), some manuscripts and presentations, personal information, and links to hobby things. Please come back and revisit this page! -As this page will continually grow through ongoing and future activities, and also me gradually digging through and recovering things from the past.

Me

Nome/名/Nómi/Ingoa: Hilário de Sousa

Iou sâm 馬交-filo. Nō Tāmaki Makaurau ahau.

Currently affiliated with Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics.

Positions held and other work experience:

  • Postdoctoral researcher at Meaning, Culture & Cognition at Radboud University Nijmegen, The Netherlands.
  • Postdoctoral researcher at Projet Sinotype at l'École des hautes études en sciences sociales, France.
  • Postdoctoral researcher at Language and Cognition at Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics, The Netherlands.
  • Coder for GramBank.
  • Linguistic consultant at Appen. (Quality control for transcription in Cantonese, and training for transcribers of Shanghainese.)
  • Linguistics tutor at The University of Sydney.
  • Group and private flute tutor in Auckland.
