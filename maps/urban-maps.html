City public transport network maps

Network maps for urban public transport are more difficult to draw than air route network maps. There are projects that I am working on at the moment (as a hobby, in my free time), and there are ones that I had finished, but I wish to totally redraw (because, e.g., my drawing skills, sense of aesthetics, understanding of usability have improved since).

I will just show you one set for now, a set which I probably won't majorly change in the future. The following is the bus network map of Greifswald. For official information, see their webpage. These are the first maps that I have drawn, with Affinity Designer. (While I am still amateurish, my skills have since improved a bit.) I wanted to try on a simpler network first, and gradually move up to more complex networks. I'll put on here other maps when I finish them.
