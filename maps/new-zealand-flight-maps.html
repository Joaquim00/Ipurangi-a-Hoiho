New Zealand — Australia/South Pacific international passenger flight route map

International non-stop flights between New Zealand and Australia/South Pacific, plus other Air New Zealand flights in the region. Regularly scheduled passenger flights, for Southern Summer 2016-2017. (Details will be checked again later as late 2016 approaches.)

Norfolk Island: After Australia abolished Norfolk Island's autonomy in July 2016, flights between Norfolk Island and Australia continue to operate through international terminals in Australia, similar to how Australia treats Christmas Island and Cocos (Keeling) Islands. A passport is strongly recommended for Australian citizens when travelling between Norfolk Island and Australia. Read carefully what Australia, Norfolk Island, and Air New Zealand say about immigration and customs to Norfolk Island, especially if you are not travelling with an Australian or New Zealand passport.
