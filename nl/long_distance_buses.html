Long-distance buses

Included here are some long-distance bus services, both domestic and international.

  • Eurolines. (See OV in Nederland's list of lines that run in the Netherlands and Belgium.)
  • FlixBus. (See OV in Nederland's list of lines that run in the Netherlands and Belgium.)
  • Air Express Bus: (Weeze Airport (Germany) ✈ –) Eindhoven Airport ✈ – Den Bosch Petterlaarpark – Utrecht P+R Westraven – Amsterdam Centraal; Eindhoven Airport ✈ – Turnhout Zwembad Stadspark (Belgium) – Antwerpen Berchem (Belgium). Printed timetables.
  • Deutsche Bahn IC Bus: Düsseldorf – Eindhoven – Antwerpen – London (webpage, brochure); Düsseldorf – Eindhoven – Antwerpen – Gent – Lille (webpage, brochure); Düsseldorf – Maastricht – Liège (webpage, brochure).
  • Ouibus (Amsterdam – Rotterdam – Brussels – Lille – Paris in various stopping patterns, and Amsterdam – Brussels – London).
  • For KLM customers with a flight to/from Amsterdam Schiphol: free bus to/from Nijmegen/Arnhem, and Maastricht/Maastricht Airport/Eindhoven Airport.
