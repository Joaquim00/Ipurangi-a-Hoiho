Domestic ferries

Water transport is very important to the Netherlans. There are far too many to list here. The following are some of the more prominent ones.

The following are the ferries that operate in 'open waters' (here it means outside all the sluice-gates, locks, and flood-barriers). Some are very frequent, while others are seasonal and do not operate every single day. All of these take at least passengers and bicycles. Check with the individual service providers. OV-chipkaart is not valid on these services unless otherwise indicated.

Northern waters:

  • Lauwersoog Lauwerseach – Schiermonnikoog Skiermûntseach: Wagenborg. Foot passengers, bicycles. Mopeds/motorcycles/cars/trucks only with permit in Schiermonnikoog. Timetables, printed timetables.
  • Holwerd Holwert – Ameland: Wagenborg. Foot passengers, bicycles, cars/trucks. Timetables, printed timetables.
  • Harlingen – Terschelling Schylge: Rederij Doeksen. Foot passengers, bicycles, cars/trucks. Printed timetable.
  • Harlingen – Vlieland: Rederij Doeksen. Foot passengers, bicycles. Mopeds/motorcycles/cars/trucks only with permit in Vlieland. Printed timetable.
  • Terschelling – Vlieland: Rederij Doeksen. Mopeds/motorcycles/cars/trucks only with permit in Vlieland. Printed timetable.
  • Vlieland – Texel: De Vriendschap. Foot passengers and bicycles. Timetable. Operates in summer.
  • Den Helder – Texel: TESO. Foot passengers, bicycles, cars/trucks. Timetable: Den Helder to Texel, Texel to Den Helder.

East coast:

  • IJmuiden – Velsen Noord [mouth of Amsterdam]: Watertaxi IJmuiden. Foot passengers and bicycles. Timetable. Operates in summer.
  • Hoek van Holland > Maasvlakte > Pistoolhaven (> Landtong) > Hoek van Holland [mouth of Rotterdam]: RET fast ferry. Foot passengers and bicycles. Printed timetables, see also the RET everything map. OV-chipkaart is not valid on RET fast ferries.
  • Vlissingen – Breskens [mouth of Antwerp]: Westerschelde Ferry. Foot passengers, bicycles, mopeds. Timetable. OV-chipkaart is valid on this service.
  • Perkpolder – Hansweert [upriver/towards Antwerp]: de-ATOL. Foot passengers, bicycles. Timetable. Operates in summer.

There are numerous river-crossing ferries in inland waters all over the country. The following are two better known operators, with networks that are better-intergrated with land-based public transport.

  • GVB ferries: GVB (English) also operates free/gratis for foot passengers, bicycles, and mopeds. The three ferries crossing the North Sea Canal downriver from (west of) Amsterdam are also free/gratis for foot passengers, bicycles, and mopeds, but larger motor vehicles have to pay, and OV-chipkaart is apparently not valid on these ferries. Timetables, maps, printed map (everything, ferries), digital map. Corresponds to concession 17 Amsterdam on land.
  • Waterbus: runs waterbuses in Rotterdam and upriver to the southeast. Timetables, printed timetables/maps. Corresponds to concession 25 Rotterdam and 26 DAV-gebied on land.

There are also many other services elsewhere. You can see a list of (basically) all foot passenger/cyclist/motor-vehicle ferries in the Netherlands, Belgium, and Luxembourg at Vrienden van de Voetveren.
