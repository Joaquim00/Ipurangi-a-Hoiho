International ferry

Long distance:

  • IJmuiden (near Amsterdam) – Newcastle upon Tyne with DFDS Seaway
  • Hoek van Holland (near Rotterdam) – Harwich with Stena Line (Hoek van Holland is Served by Hoek van Holland Haven train station.)
  • Europoort Rotterdam – Hull with P&O Ferries
  • Zeebrugge (Belgium) – Hull with P&O Ferries (Walking from the ferry terminal to the two Belgian train stations in Zeebrugge not recommended)

Short distance:

  • In Europe:
    • AG Ems: Eemshaven – Borkum (Germany). Timetable.
    • Internationale Dollard Route: Delfzijl – Emden (Germany) – Ditzum (Germany). Operates in summer.
    • AG Ems: Delfzijl – Emden-Knock (German) – Borkum (Germany). Operates in summer. Reservation compulsory.
  • In Caribbean: details from the tourism websites of Sint Eustatius, Saba.

In Europe, there are numerous river cruises to/from Belgium and Germany, some even to/from France, Switzerland, or further (e.g. canal to the Danube). In the Caribbean, there are numerous Caribbean cruiships that visit Bonaire.
