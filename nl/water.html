Water

Long distance international ferries are all to England, and there are lots of ferries flying the inland waters of the Netherlands. Please click one of the links below on various topics concerning the ground public transport in the Netherlands.

A handy electronic journey planner for all public transport in the Netherlands is 9292.nl (App: Google Play, Apple App Store, BlackBerry World.)

In case you need to make a complaint, you can make them to the public transport provider, or OV Loket, ombudsman for public transport in the Netherlands. For more serious matters, contact the Politie in the Netherlands. (Politie in Caribbean Netherlands.)
