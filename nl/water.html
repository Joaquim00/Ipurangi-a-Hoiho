Water

There are many cruise ships that visit the Netherlands. There are long-distance international ferries to England, and short-distance ferries across to parts of Germany and Belgium. Many ferries ply the inland waters of the Netherlands. Please click one of the links below.

A handy electronic journey planner for nearly all public transport in the Netherlands is 9292.nl (App: Google Play, Apple App Store, BlackBerry World.)

In case you need to make a complaint, you can make them to the public transport provider, or OV Loket, ombudsman for public transport in the Netherlands. For more serious matters, contact the Politie in the Netherlands. (Politie in Caribbean Netherlands.)

Public transport:

  • International ferries
  • Domestic ferries

Veerbootinfo.nl is a website giving information on, e.g., individual vessels, of the larger ferry companies that operate between Mainland Netherlands and the Frisian Islands or England. A small number of ferries accept the OV-chipkaart (see the payment on domestic public transport page).
