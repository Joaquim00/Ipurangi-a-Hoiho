Surface public transport

There are many different public transport options on the ground or on water. Please click one of the links below on various topics concerning the surface public transport in the Netherlands, or the last link for a brief summary on the surface public transport in Belgium and Luxembourg.

A handy electronic journey planner for all public transport in the Netherlands is 9292.nl (App: Google Play, Apple App Store, BlackBerry World.) The following pages are more aimed at understanding entire networks, printed network maps, and other related information (i.e., geekiness).

In case you need to make a complaint, you can make them to the public transport provider, or OV Loket, ombudsman for public transport in the Netherlands. For more serious matters, contact the Politie in the Netherlands. (Politie in Caribbean Netherlands).
