How to go to/from/within The Netherlands

This website called 'How to NL' is about some of the more common ways of travelling to/from/within The Netherlands that I can think of. Perhaps in the future I can say something about ways of doing other things in the Netherlands.

If you just want to know how to get from A to B within the Netherlands by public transport, a handy website is 9292.nl (app: Google Play, Apple App Store, BlackBerry World).

I try to describe what I know, and provide links to official sources of information as much as possible. However, I cannot guarantee the accuracy of the information that I or they provide. Information can easily go out of date.

I am not a travel agency. Don't ask me questions like 'how can I cancel my train ticket from Schiphol to Brussels', or 'can I catch a ferry from Nijmegen to Ittoqqortoormiit', or 'where can I take my horse for hoof trimming in the Netherlands'. You can ask Google. (I am very helpful in general; but I cannot guarantee you an answer.)

Currently, the pages are quite text-heavy; I will endeavour to have more diagrams and photos amongst the texts.
