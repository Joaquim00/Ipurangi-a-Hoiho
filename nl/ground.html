Ground transport

There are many different public transport options on the ground, and some underground. There are also taxis and carpooling, and many DIY options for ground transportation. Please click one of the links below.

A handy electronic journey planner for all public transport in the Netherlands is 9292.nl (App: Google Play, Apple App Store, BlackBerry World.) The following pages are more aimed at understanding entire networks, printed network maps, and other related information (i.e., geekiness).

In case you need to make a complaint, you can make them to the public transport provider, or OV Loket, ombudsman for public transport in the Netherlands. For more serious matters, contact the Politie in the Netherlands.

See also the Payment on domestic public transport page, and the Public transport in Belgium, Luxembourg, and nearby Germany page.
